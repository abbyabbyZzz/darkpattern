// WHAT ARE DARK PATTERNS?

DESIGN THAT
TRAPS YOU.

Dark patterns are interface tricks — deliberate design choices that push users toward actions they didn't intend. Named by UX researcher Harry Brignull in 2010, they're everywhere.

01 🔒

ROACH MOTEL

Easy to subscribe, nearly impossible to cancel. Settings buried five menus deep. Phone-only cancellation. Loops that never end.

IN OUR GAME
02 💔

CONFIRMSHAMING

Cancel buttons labelled "No thanks, I hate saving money." Guilt-trip copy designed to make you second-guess a perfectly reasonable choice.

IN OUR GAME
03 🤖

FAKE LIVE CHAT

A "support agent" that's actually a bot — stalling, deflecting, wearing you down until you give up on cancelling entirely.

IN OUR GAME
04 ⏱

FALSE URGENCY

Countdown timers and "limited time" banners on every visit — manufacturing pressure for a decision that was never urgent at all.

IN OUR GAME
