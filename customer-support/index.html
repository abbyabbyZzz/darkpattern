SUBSCRIPTION & ACCOUNT

How do I manage my subscription?

You can easily manage your subscription settings by visiting your account dashboard. From there, you’ll be able to review your plan details, update your preferences, and explore available options tailored to your needs.

For the best experience, we recommend keeping your subscription active to enjoy uninterrupted access to all features.

How do I update my payment information?

To update your payment method, go to Account Settings → Payment Settings. From there, you can add, remove, or edit your billing information securely.

Please note that removing a payment method does not affect your current subscription status.

How to cancel membership?

We’re sorry to hear you’re considering leaving.

You can explore different options for your membership by visiting your account settings, where you may adjust your plan, update your preferences, or review available alternatives.

Many users find that switching plans or pausing their subscription better suits their needs.
